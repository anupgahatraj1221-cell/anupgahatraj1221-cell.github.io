Dell Pro 15 Essential

Introduction

The Dell Pro 15 Essential is a 15-inch productivity laptop designed for business use with a durable chassis and a full-size keyboard featuring a numeric keypad. It offers a 15.6-inch full HD (1920×1080) IPS display with up to 120 Hz refresh rate, and options for Intel Core processors up to 13th Gen i7 or AMD Ryzen 7000 series chips, paired with up to 32 GB of RAM and up to 1 TB PCIe NVMe SSD storage. It runs Windows 11 (Pro or Home) and includes integrated graphics, multiple ports including USB-A, USB-C, HDMI 1.4, an SD card reader, Wi-Fi 5/6, Bluetooth, and an HD or FHD camera. The laptop is available with a plastic or optional aluminum chassis in colors like Carbon Black or Platinum Silver, and weighs around 1.9 kg, making it suitable for everyday office tasks, web browsing, and standard productivity workflows.
